Mozilla Firefox

Mozilla Firefox is an open-source web browser developed by the Mozilla Foundation, first released in 2002. Firefox is the successor to the earlier Netscape Navigator, which was one of the most popular browsers in the 1990s. Extensions are one of Firefox's strongest features; additional functionality such as adblocking, userstyles, and enhanced privacy features can be added with just a few clicks.
